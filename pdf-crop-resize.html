Frequently Asked Questions
Can I crop specific pages differently? ▼
You can set crop dimensions that apply to all pages. For different crops per page, process pages individually using Split PDF first.
Is the PDF crop tool free with no watermark? ▼
Completely free, no watermark, and no sign-up required at ConvertPDF.Live.
Can I resize a PDF to A4 from Letter size? ▼
Yes — choose A4 or Letter as the target page size and all pages will be resized to that standard dimension.
Is my PDF kept private during cropping? ▼
Yes — all cropping and resizing is done locally in your browser using PDF-lib. Your file is never uploaded to any server.
Will cropping remove content permanently? ▼
Yes — cropped content is removed from the downloaded PDF. Keep a copy of your original before cropping.
