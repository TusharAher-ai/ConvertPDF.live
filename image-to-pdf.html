Frequently Asked Questions

Is Image to PDF Converter really free? ▾
Yes, 100% free. No account, no watermarks, no limits — all processing runs in your browser.
Do my images get uploaded? ▾
Never. All images are processed locally using JavaScript. They never leave your device.
How many images can I convert? ▾
Up to 40 images per conversion. Add more batches if you need more pages.
Can I reorder images before converting? ▾
Yes — drag and drop the image thumbnails to rearrange the page order before downloading.
Which image formats are supported? ▾
JPG (JPEG), PNG, and WebP. For best quality use PNG for graphics and JPG for photos.
What page sizes are available? ▾
Auto (fits image), A4, A3, Letter, and Square. You can also set the orientation and margin.
