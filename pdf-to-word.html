Frequently Asked Questions
Why might formatting differ from the original? ▼
Browser-based extraction reads text streams, not layout. For exact replication of complex layouts, a desktop tool with server processing is needed.
What if my PDF is scanned? ▼
Enable OCR above. Tesseract.js will render each page and recognise the text.
Are files uploaded? ▼
Never. 100% processed in your browser.
