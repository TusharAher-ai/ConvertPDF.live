Frequently Asked Questions
Is my PDF file safe? ▼
Yes — 100%. All conversion happens entirely in your browser using PDF.js and docx.js. Your file is never sent to any server or stored anywhere. When you close the tab, everything is gone.
Will formatting be preserved? ▼
Yes. We extract font families, sizes, weights, colors, paragraph spacing, alignment, tables, and images directly from the PDF's internal structure and reconstruct them accurately in the .docx output. Complex multi-column layouts are linearized in reading order.
What about scanned PDFs? ▼
Scanned (image-only) PDFs are automatically detected. When OCR is enabled, Tesseract.js runs in the background to extract text from each page image. It supports 100+ languages including English, Hindi, Arabic, Chinese, and more.
What is the maximum file size? ▼
Up to 50 MB for free. Very large PDFs (100+ pages) may take longer to process on slower devices — the progress bar keeps you informed throughout.
Can I convert password-protected PDFs? ▼
Yes. If a password-protected PDF is detected, you'll be prompted to enter the password. It is used locally to unlock the PDF and is never stored or transmitted.
What if my PDF is corrupted or damaged? ▼
The converter handles corrupted PDFs gracefully, extracting as much content as possible. If a page fails, it is skipped with a note, and remaining pages are still converted.
