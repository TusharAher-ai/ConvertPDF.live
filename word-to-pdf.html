Frequently Asked Questions

Is Word to PDF Converter really free? ▾
Yes, 100% free. No account needed, no watermarks, and no file size limits imposed by us — everything runs in your browser.
Does my Word file get uploaded to a server? ▾
Never. Your .docx file is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. It never leaves your device.
What formatting is preserved? ▾
Text content, headings (H1–H6), bold and italic text, bullet and numbered lists, and paragraph breaks are preserved. Complex elements like embedded images, tables, text boxes, and advanced Word styles may not render exactly as in the original document.
Which file formats are supported? ▾
The converter supports .docx files (Microsoft Word 2007 and later). Older .doc format is not supported — please save your document as .docx first in Word.
Which browsers are supported? ▾
Any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari on desktop and mobile. No plugins or extensions required.
