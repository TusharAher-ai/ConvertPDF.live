Frequently Asked Questions

Is Excel to PDF Converter free? ▾
Yes, 100% free. No account needed, no watermarks, no file size limits imposed by us.
Does my spreadsheet get uploaded? ▾
Never. SheetJS reads your file entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
Which file formats are supported? ▾
.xlsx (Excel 2007+), .xls (older Excel), .csv and .ods are all supported.
Can I convert multiple sheets? ▾
Yes — choose 'All sheets' in the options to convert every sheet in the workbook. Each sheet is rendered on its own page.
What does the table preview show? ▾
A preview of the first 50 rows of the selected sheet is shown before conversion so you can verify the data looks correct.
Are formulas and formatting preserved? ▾
Calculated values from formulas are preserved. Cell formatting (colors, fonts, borders) is approximated — complex Excel formatting may not be reproduced exactly.
